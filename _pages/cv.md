Profile
======
Environmental Engineer with an M.Sc. in Physics from the National University of Colombia, currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering at the Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC), Brazil. My research focuses on the synthesis and catalytic design of functional materials for hydrogen production and oxygen transport, with strong experience in the synthesis and characterization of semiconductor perovskites and related nanostructured materials. I am especially interested in applying this background to perovskite-based solar cell research, bridging catalysis, energy conversion, and advanced materials engineering.

Education
======
* **Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering**, Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC), Florianópolis, Brazil — *Sep. 2024 – Aug. 2028 (in progress)*
  * Focus: new catalytic materials for hydrogen production and oxygen transport.
* **M.Sc. in Sciences – Physics**, National University of Colombia, Bogotá D.C. — *Aug. 2022 – Dec. 2024*
  * Focus: physics of materials, solid state, and modeling of materials for environmental applications.
* **M.Sc. in Environmental Sciences**, University of Cádiz, Spain — *Sep. 2019 – Jul. 2021*
  * Focus: modeling and estimation of greenhouse gases and their radiative effects on the atmosphere.
* **Environmental Engineering**, National University of Colombia, Palmira (Valle del Cauca) — *Aug. 2013 – May 2019*
  * Focus: synthesis and characterization of TiO₂ for catalytic applications and photocatalysis.
