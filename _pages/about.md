I am **Diego Alejandro Rueda Cadavid**, an Environmental Engineer with an M.Sc. in Physics from the [National University of Colombia](https://unal.edu.co/) and an M.Sc. in Environmental Sciences from the [University of Cádiz](https://www.uca.es/) (Spain). I am currently a **PhD candidate in Chemical Engineering** at the [Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC)](https://procer.ufsc.br/), in Florianópolis, Brazil, supported by a CAPES scholarship.

My research focuses on the **synthesis and catalytic design of functional materials for hydrogen production and oxygen transport**. I have strong hands-on experience in the synthesis and characterization of semiconductor perovskites and related nanostructured materials. I am especially interested in extending this background toward **perovskite-based solar cell research**, bridging catalysis, energy conversion, and advanced materials engineering.

Research interests
======
* Synthesis and characterization of perovskites and double perovskites
* Photocatalysis and photoreforming for green hydrogen production
* Oxygen transport and oxygen-vacancy engineering in oxide materials
* Nanostructured semiconductors (TiO₂, manganites) for environmental and energy applications
* Bibliometrics, text-mining and machine learning applied to materials science
