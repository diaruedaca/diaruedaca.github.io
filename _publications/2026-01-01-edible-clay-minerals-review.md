## Summary

This mixed systematic–narrative review examines edible clay minerals and related layered materials (such as natural and modified clays) used to load and deliver bioactive compounds in foods. It discusses the interaction mechanisms between the layered hosts and the bioactives, the main food-related applications, and the safety considerations associated with their ingestion, combining a systematic literature search with a narrative synthesis of the field.
